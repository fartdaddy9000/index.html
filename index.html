Genshin Impact

This game is an open-world, action game that takes place in the fantasy world of Teyvat. In this game you are able to explore the nations of Teyvat and fight lots of enemies, all while being immersed in the beautiful storyline. You can also wish / pull for your favourite characters using "fates" or "primogems".

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • It's fun to explore around the map.
  • I am able to meet new players from all over Asia.
  • Fun way to pass time.
  • I like to fight the new enemies ^_^.
  • It holds alot of meaning to me as my friends and I loved playing during quarantine.
